Join the Pittsburgh Pharaoh Hounds and Gingerbread Man Running at the 9th Annual Petite Track Meet on Friday, June 27th, 2025, starting at 6 PM.

The Petite Track Meet is an open track meet for everyone in Pittsburgh! All ages and running abilities are welcome!

[REGISTRATION IS LIVE for PTM 2025] and closes Friday, June 27th, at 6 PM, so don't delay!

TIP: Register as an "Unattached Athlete" unless you are part of a running club/team that has a profile on athletic.net or want to pre-register for the 4x400 relay (see FAQs)

Registration is $20 + processing fee and includes all the events you'd like to run!

Come run your PR in a variety of races -- from 100 m to 5000 m -- at a convenient location at Schenley Park.

FAQs


Q: How do I register for the 4x400 relay?
4x400 relay registration is available online only if you are connected with a "team" in your athletic.net profile. If you want to register online for the 4x400 relay, make sure your profile is connected to a team (if desired, you can connect with the Pittsburgh Pharaoh Hounds team). But don't worry: if you are unable to register for the 4x400 relay online, you can just register a team at the registration tent during the meet as the 4x400 approaches; listen for announcements at the meet for details.
